Xianzhe Meng

I am a sophomore undergraduate student majoring in Automation at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Automation, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zhigang Zeng.

My research interests lie in optimization theory, AI for Science, and reinforcement learning. I am deeply curious about replacing traditional heuristic approaches with precise, math-based algorithms, as well as designing novel machine learning architectures that break through existing structural bottlenecks.
